Who I Am

Driven by Purpose
& Technology

I'm Getachew Dires Alebachew, a Computer Science graduate from the University of Gondar — one of Ethiopia's premier academic institutions. My academic journey equipped me with a strong foundation in software engineering, algorithms, database systems, and computer networks.

Today, I serve as a National ID Supervisor at Ethiopost, where I play a central role in Ethiopia's national digital identity infrastructure — the Fayda program. I oversee citizen enrollment operations, manage registration teams, ensure data quality and compliance, and contribute directly to the country's vision of a fully digitized identity ecosystem.

In 2023, I completed an industrial practice at the Commercial Bank of Ethiopia (CBE) — one of the continent's largest financial institutions — gaining deep exposure to enterprise banking technology, core banking systems, and large-scale data management in a high-stakes environment.

I believe technology is most powerful when it solves real human problems at scale. From national identity to financial infrastructure, I am committed to building and supporting systems that matter.
